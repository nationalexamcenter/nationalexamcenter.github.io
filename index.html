Welcome to National Exam Center ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

🚫 3. Fraud, Cheating, and Abuse Prevention

⚠️ Important: Consequences of Fraudulent Activity

National Exam Center is committed to maintaining a fair and honest learning environment. Any attempt to cheat, manipulate the system, or engage in fraudulent activity will result in immediate action.

3.1 What Constitutes Cheating/Fraud

  • Coin Manipulation: Attempting to artificially increase coin balances through unauthorized means
  • Time Manipulation: Altering device time to bypass cooldown periods or daily limits
  • Multiple Accounts: Creating multiple guest accounts to exceed free topic limits
  • Automated Tools: Using bots, scripts, or automated tools to interact with the app
  • Exploiting Bugs: Intentionally exploiting system vulnerabilities for unfair advantage
  • Ad Fraud: Attempting to fake ad views or clicks
  • Progress Manipulation: Falsifying study time or quiz scores
  • Unauthorized Access: Attempting to access another user's account

3.2 Detection Methods

We employ various automated and manual methods to detect fraudulent activity, including:

  • Automated cheat detection algorithms
  • Time anomaly detection
  • Pattern analysis for suspicious activity
  • Device fingerprinting to prevent multiple guest accounts
  • Regular audits of coin transactions and usage patterns

3.3 Consequences of Violations

First-Time Minor Offenses

  • Temporary Ban: 7-day account suspension
  • Coin Reset: All coins reset to zero
  • Warning: Formal warning sent to registered email

Repeat or Major Offenses

  • Extended Temporary Ban: 30-day account suspension
  • Progress Reset: All study progress and quiz scores reset
  • Feature Restriction: Limited access to certain features

Severe or Malicious Violations

  • Permanent Ban: Account permanently deactivated
  • Device Blacklist: Device ID blacklisted from creating new accounts
  • No Refund: Any paid services forfeited without refund
  • Legal Action: For severe cases involving fraud or unauthorized access

Examples of severe violations: Attempting to hack the system, distributing cheats, ad fraud, or malicious attacks on our services.

3.4 Appeal Process

If you believe your account was banned in error, you may appeal by contacting us with:

  • Your account email
  • Date of ban
  • Explanation of why you believe it was a mistake
  • Any supporting evidence

Appeals are reviewed within 3-5 business days. Decisions are final.
